Payments, Refunds & Credits

Payments

Deposits: To secure your Tour Only booking, a deposit—paid via one of our accepted payment methods—is required and will be applied toward your package cost. If your tour includes airfare, the required deposit amount may be higher. Deposits vary depending on the package, departure date, and city. For exact deposit requirements, check the package page, start a booking, or contact us. Customizations or changes to packages may require an increased deposit. Once your booking is confirmed, deposits are generally non-refundable unless covered by our Carefree Booking policy or if we cancel your departure.

When adding Travel Protection coverage during booking, the premium will be included in your deposit.

Final Payment: If there’s a balance remaining after your initial deposit, it must be paid in full by the final payment due date. This date typically falls 75 days before departure and is outlined during booking, in your receipt email, under Your Booking, and on your invoice. Partial payments can be made at any time until the due date. Late payments will incur a $50 fee, and bookings unpaid 21 days after the due date will be canceled, forfeiting the deposit. Overpayments can be refunded or applied as Travel Credits without expiration.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Credit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express are accepted for deposits or balances, either online or via phone with an agent. Multiple cards may be used by contacting us directly.
  • PayPal: Payments can be made via your PayPal account on our website.
  • Checks: Personal checks, cashier’s checks, or money orders from U.S. institutions are accepted. Include your booking number on the check and mail it to our office. For deposit payments via check, we recommend calling us first to start your booking and then mailing the check promptly to ensure space and pricing availability.
  • Travel Credits: If you have Travel Credits issued by us, contact us to redeem them.

Payments are accepted globally in multiple currencies via the payment methods mentioned above. Currency conversion fees may apply depending on the payment method and location.

Returned Payments

If a check is returned due to insufficient funds, stop payment, or other reasons, a $50 fee will be added to your balance. Alternate payment arrangements must be made before the final due date. If a returned check is reported after the final payment due date, a replacement payment is required within 48 hours to avoid booking cancellation.

Refunds

Refunds are processed as quickly as possible, typically within six weeks of the request. If we must recover funds from our partners, the refund will be issued after those funds are secured. Payments made by credit card will be refunded to the same card, provided the refund doesn’t exceed the amount paid on that card; otherwise, refunds will be issued via check.

Travel Credits

Travel Credits may be issued for reasons like cancellations, referrals, promotions, or other situations. Some credits come with expiration dates, while others do not. Credits must be applied to a booking made before they expire, though travel does not need to occur before the expiration date. Expired credits cannot be redeemed.

Credits are typically issued to the individual who made the booking or the person who referred a new traveler. They can be transferred to another person by notifying us in writing.

Credits are issued in U.S. dollars and can be applied toward future bookings but cannot be used for visa fees, optional excursions, airport fees, or other items not payable to Friendly Planet. Credits cannot be exchanged for cash, and unused credits will remain available for future bookings, provided they have not expired.

Changes

Carefree Booking

The Carefree Booking policy applies exclusively to new bookings that do not use funds from previously canceled reservations. This policy is limited to your original booking, allowing only one rebooking. Subsequent bookings will follow standard cancellation policies, and discounts or coupons from the canceled booking will not transfer to the new one.

If you decide to cancel for any reason before your final payment due date, you can:

  • Rebook: Apply all payments, including travel insurance premiums, towards a new departure on the same or a different package for the same or greater number of travelers.
  • Travel Credits: Receive Travel Credits for the full amount paid (excluding insurance premiums). These credits are valid for 5 years and can be transferred.
  • Refund with Travel Credits: Get a refund for all payments made, minus $199 per person issued as Travel Credits, valid for 5 years.

Important Notes:

  • Airline change or cancellation fees may apply if special flight arrangements were requested, or if airline tickets were issued for an earlier booking before being transferred to the new one.
  • Cancellations after the final payment due date are subject to standard cancellation fees.
  • The Carefree Booking policy does not apply to all packages (e.g., private groups, custom group departures, and Biblical Journeys). Check the package page for eligibility.

Cancellations

Cancellations by You

If you choose to cancel your booking, you must notify us in writing. Unless covered by our Carefree Booking policy, cancellations are subject to fees that vary based on your booking date, cancellation date, and package. These fees are outlined in the cancellation schedule effective at the time of your booking. The applicable schedule can be found under "Your Booking" or attached to your booking receipt email.

Upon cancellation, we will refund all payments made, minus:

  • Your deposit or the applicable cancellation fee, whichever is greater.
  • The cost of issued flight tickets if they are nonrefundable, or any cancellation fee assessed by the airline if refundable.

When you request a cancellation, we will calculate and inform you of the total fees and any refundable amount. In some cases, part of the cancellation fee may be converted into a Travel Credit, which does not expire and can be applied to any future booking.

If you booked for multiple travelers, one or more travelers may cancel while allowing others to continue. Cancellation fees will only apply to the canceling travelers. However, if the change results in a double occupancy room or cabin being converted to single occupancy, the remaining traveler(s) must cover the single occupancy rate. In some instances, a new traveler may replace a canceled traveler, subject to a name change fee and any applicable airline or vendor charges for issued flight tickets.

Passports, Visas & Entry Requirements

We provide guidance for Kenyan citizens on entry requirements for destinations we offer. Travelers with passports from other countries must verify the passport, visa, and entry requirements relevant to them. As requirements often change without notice, it’s crucial to confirm with the respective embassy or consulate. Ultimately, each traveler is responsible for securing necessary travel documents, meeting all entry requirements, and covering any associated Entry & Exit Fees. We are not liable for interruptions, cancellations, or expenses due to non-compliance.

Passports

A valid passport is required for international travel. Kenyan travelers should ensure their passports are valid for at least six months beyond the return date and contain sufficient blank visa pages. Failure to meet these criteria could result in denied entry. Passport details, including exact names and titles, must match those provided during booking to avoid travel disruptions. Corrections made after booking could incur additional costs. For some packages, color copies of passport photo/signature pages must be submitted within 5 days of booking or with final payment.

Travel Visas & Other Documents

Many destinations require travel visas. Some may also require proof of immunization or additional documentation for minors traveling without parents or with parents of different surnames. We provide visa instructions for Kenyan citizens upon booking as a courtesy. Travelers with other passports must independently determine their visa requirements.

Entry & Exit Fees

Certain countries, airports, or ports charge entry or exit fees payable directly by travelers. These fees are not included in our package prices and may require payment in cash or by credit card, depending on the destination. While we strive to inform you of such fees in advance, omissions or errors may occur, and we cannot be held responsible.
